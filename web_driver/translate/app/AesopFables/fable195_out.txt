Fable 195

The Sow(母豬) and the Lioness(母獅)

The story goes that a sow who had delivered(分娩) a whole litter(產仔) of piglets(小豬) loudly accosted(上前搭訕) a lioness. ‘How many children do you breed(繁殖)?’ asked the sow. ‘I breed only one,’ said the lioness, ‘but he is very well bred!’

The fable shows that a single man who is remarkable(非凡的) for physical strength and bravery(勇敢) and wisdom(智慧) is mightier(偉大的) than many weak and foolish people. 

accosted （貿然）上前搭訕；（唐突地）走近談話 
bravery 勇敢，英勇，英勇行為

mightier 強大的；巨大的；偉大的 
piglets 小豬，豬仔 
remarkable 非凡的；奇異的；引人注目的 
sow 母豬 
wisdom 智慧；明智
